What changed in the plan:
- The inner Index Scan on dim_repos_pkey is gone.
- Group Key flipped from r.org_name to f.org_name (no more join).
- Buffer hits dropped from 139,144 to 4,848 (~29x less I/O).
- Execution time roughly halved (115.98 ms -> 58.16 ms).

The Sort + GroupAggregate is still there because Postgres can't avoid sorting on org_name to group by it — but it's now sorting rows that already carry the column, instead of doing 33,574 lookups first.
